About Me

I’m a front-end developer with a BSc (Honours) degree in Software Development from Munster Technological University (Ireland). I focus on building clean, responsive, and user-friendly websites using modern web technologies.

I care about structure, performance, and details — from layout and typography to subtle interactions that make a site feel polished. Whether it’s a landing page, portfolio, or a small business website, I aim to build things that look good and work smoothly on any device.

Currently, I’m focused on improving my front-end skills, building real projects, and refining my design sense through hands-on work.
